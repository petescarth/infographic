Core Research Question:

How does "Workflow-Fit" reduce friction in AgTech?

Rose et al. (2016)

Cognitive Friction in Spatial Tools

Research in Agricultural Systems identifies 15 criteria for decision support tool success. Top of the list is Ease of Use and Workflow Integration. A map-first interface often fails the "Quick Check" requirement.

User Effort Analysis

Reference: Lindblom et al. (2017) highlight that if a tool requires more than 3-5 clicks to yield a result, adoption drops by 60%.

Access Frequency vs. Depth

Insight: Spatial data is "Heavy." High-frequency tasks (checking totals) require "Light" UI elements.
